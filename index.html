About Me

I wasn´t alsways a tech enthusiast, but that changed in 2017 when I was offered a job at an Online Shop as an office assistant. Shortly into the job my boss discovered my talent for details and creativity and so she gave me the free hand to express it and soon I was doing the job of an Online Marketing Manager without realising it.

I discovered the vast possibilities embedded in the cyber space and I didn´t need to be an expert to realise that, that is where the future lies and there I promissed myself that I was going to get deeper into it than just what I knew back then.
The first opportunity presented itself in 2019 where I was offered a placement at WBS to hone my softskills. It was a six months intensive Business Administration course and two of those were dedicated to MS Office. And as a follow up, I did another six months intensive online marketing course at the same institution in 2020 where I trained to become an Online Marketing Manager.

But I didn´t stop there. I embarked on a self-learning journey to becoming a programmer in 2021 while at the same time working as a sales rep at an insurance firm. I liked my job, but it wasn´t what I really wanted, so the firm and I agreed to end our working relationship and in January I applied for the Web Development course at Neue Fische, which is where I currently am, and I was delighted to be accepted to this course that is bound to open many prospects, because my aim is to become a Java Developer, and I hope that I´ll also get an admission to the Java Development course in this same institution after this one.
